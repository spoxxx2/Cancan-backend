A living civic organism in Kern County

CANCANKERN is a daily civic care system built and operated by Casey Canfield. Every day, the organism documents neighborhood conditions, responds to needs, and publishes transparent public proof through the Dashboard and DOI Archive.

This site is the public home of the organism — updated continuously from the field.
